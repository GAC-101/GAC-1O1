Introduction To Courses

First Semester

GAC008 Add Lang to Spanish

  1. This subject could be the equivalent to English. Every day you will be doing summaries, outlines, and tasks. It is not a hard subject, but it requires concentration, time, and effort.

GAC003 Research Methodology

  1. This is a class that will teach you to be decisive about your future, making a curriculum and being interviewed are examples of ways to help you know your vocation and this subject will help to achieve it.

GAC006 Multiculturalism

  1. In this subject you will learn about yourself and other people, topics like the mbti test and types of intelligence will be explained, there will be a lot of tasks for you to answer but do not worry it is a fun subject, try your hardest!

GAC007 Add Lang to Spanish

  1. At first you might be confused about this subject since its partner, GAC008, has the same subject name, however, this one focuses more on writing, generally the works will be from GAC008, although there are specific tasks and assessments from this subject, it is not a worry, since it is so little anyway.

GAC005 Informatics

  1. Here you will learn about GAC formats, the Global Assessment Certificate, also has its own specific way of presenting a project (which from now on will be named as ´Assessment´ and can be shorten to ´AE´) and referencing, it is very important to listen and understand to what will be explained; you will also have small class activities, those can be done in pairs or alone.

GAC004 Mathematics

  1. This subject is very straightforward, you will review topics from middle school but with more complexity, this subject also includes a long exam with everything that you saw in the semester and handmade projects that require excel, so be sure to give your best.
